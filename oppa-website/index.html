About Us

What is ACC? A group of consultants with a lot of experience in solving R&D and IT challenges. We cover a wide range of expertise, and if we cannot help you, we will be happy to refer you to someone in our extended network that will.

Our experience includes:

Successfully bringing engineering organizations through complex transitions

IPOs, acquisitions (both sides), merging / splitting teams working on different products, scaling up rapidly, scaling down effectively, adjusting to a major strategic shift inside of the company

Defining product roadmaps and/or technology strategy

  • We have considerable experience in this area. We can help prioritize projects and initiatives. We can deal with the complexity and ambiguity that is inherent in most situations, help the R&D leadership team define an actionable plan and provide clarity and high-level direction to team members.
  • We are part of an extended network of Product Managers and Technical Experts that cover a wide range of technologies

Helping engineering teams become more effective and productive

  • We have driven or coached R&D teams to achieve success in environments where resources were scarce, challenges many, and the pressure to deliver extremely high.
  • We can help teams operate more effectively through process improvements as well as the identification and resolution of organizational dysfunctions.

  • Full product cycle / SDLC, development, quality assurance, sustaining, NPI, etc.
  • We believe the right process can greatly improve quality, productivity, visibility and predictability. To accomplish this, it needs to bring structure but cannot stifle creativity or slow things down. We go beyond the conventional approach (which does not always work for everyone, particularly in organizations that develop system software or hardware). Improve quality and customer experience for products and services

  • We have achieved significant improvements via process and organizational changes
  • it is not enough to have products and services that are robust and dependable, it is also important to deliver something that is easy to use and can be supported effectively in the field (all the way from pre-sales to break / fix).
